History of Cyber Security

Cyber security began as a simple method of protecting standalone computers but has now become a global necessity. With the growth of the internet, cyber crimes such as hacking, phishing, and identity theft have increased rapidly. Today, cyber security protects everything from banking systems to social media accounts, ensuring safe communication and data protection. Governments and organizations invest heavily in cyber security to prevent attacks and secure sensitive information.
